The power of Jesus Christ heal … great who gave birth to D---- from all sickness and pain of head and temples and fever and fever with shivering fits.

Notes

  • Fragment 1 verso, line 1: The symbols on each side of the first line are tau-rho staurograms (⳨).
  • Fragment 1 verso, line 2: Possibly θεραπευσον μεγαν.
  • Fragment 1 verso, line 3: See Lk 5:15; 6:18; 7:4; 8:2.
  • Fragment 1 verso, line 4: For κοτραφων read κροταφων.
  • Fragment 1 verso, line 5: For ριγωπυρετου read ριγοπυρετου.
