Notes

Fragment 1 recto, line 1: Editors suggest possibly η εκκλησ[ια] at the end of the line.

Fragment 1 recto, line 2: Editors suggest possibly κ(αι) εμε σωσει at start of line based on Ps 137:7 [LXX]

Fragment 1 recto, line 3: Editors suggest δεκατ may be δεκα +

Fragment 1 recto, line 5: Editors suggest the line start may be η ουσια and reject ημισια.

Fragment 1 recto, line 7: Editors suggest λυμψας may instead be λαμψας. Also possibly στηρισον τας καρδιας (cf. Ps 50:14 [LXX]; 111:8 [LXX])

Fragment 1 recto, line 9: See Ps 137:5 [LXX]. For αυτοκρατωρ, see 4Ma. 1:7, 13, 30; 8:28; 16:1

Fragment 1 recto, line 11: See Ps 28:11 [LXX]. For αωνα, read αιωνα.

Fragment 1 recto, line 12: Relies on Ps 47:1–2 [LXX]. For ενετο read αινετος.

Fragment 1 recto, line 14: See Ps 47:9 [LXX]. For οικουσαμεν read possibly ηκουσαμεν. For ιδαμεν read ειδομεν.

Fragment 1 recto, line 15: There is extended supralinear material between lines 14 and 15, and lines 15 and 16, but where it integrates into the main material is unclear.

Fragment 1 recto, line 16: For σοι read σου; see Ps 34:24 [LXX]; Ps 51:11 [LXX]. There is extended supralinear material between lines 14 and 15, and lines 15 and 16, but where it integrates into the main material is unclear. In the running translation, the supralinear material is inserted as a whole after line 15 as this seems to make the most sense, but it is still a guess.

Fragment 1 verso, line 19: Editors tentatively suggest a verb like σω]σον.

Fragment 1 verso, line 21: See Is 26:13.

Fragment 1 verso, line 24: For ημας read ημιν. See Ps 78:9 [LXX].

Fragment 1 verso, line 27: Relies on Is 38:20. For πισμαι read παυσομαι.

Fragment 1 verso, line 30: See Ps 144:15–16 [LXX]

Fragment 1 verso, line 32: For επιπλας read εμπιπλας. For τος αγαθος read τοις αγαθοις. For χιρα read χειρα. For ανυξον I have read ανοιγεις based on the parallel with Ps 144:16 [LXX].
