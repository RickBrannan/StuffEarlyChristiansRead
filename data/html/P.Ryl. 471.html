+ The holy oil of gladness against every adverse power and for the grafting of your good olive tree of the catholic and apostolic church. εφτε Amen +

Notes

  • Fragment 1 recto, line 1: For ελεον read ελαιον. Cf. Ps 44:8 [LXX]; Heb 1:9.
  • Fragment 1 recto, line 3: For ενεργιας read ενεργειας. Cf. Rom 11:17–24
  • Fragment 1 recto, line 4: For καλλιελεαυ read καλλιελαιου.
  • Fragment 1 recto, line 5: Roberts notes that he has no explanation for the letters εφτε. While the φ is marked as uncertain, Roberts notes it is the most probable reading.
