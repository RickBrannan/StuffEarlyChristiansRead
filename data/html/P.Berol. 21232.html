Notes

  • Fragment 1 recto, line 1: For ερεσις read αιρεσις. For τρ.., Treu prefers τρο̣π̣ but acknowledges τρε̣ι̣ and τρε̣τ̣ as possibilities.
  • Fragment 1 recto, line 2: τ‘ε’α = τα corrected to τε. For αρια̣τικο̣ς (cf. Tt 3:10), Treu notes αριν̣τικο̣ς is also possible.
  • Fragment 1 recto, line 3: For ομοτοξον read ομοδοξον.
  • Fragment 1 recto, line 4: For ειχει read εχει. Treu suggests that .[...]οθις could be σ[αρκ]ωθεις if line 6 σταυροθις indicates an instance of a Trisagion formula, but he then discounts the suggestion as not likely. For καρ read γαρ.
  • Fragment 1 recto, line 5: The character between πο and σας looks like a superscript majuscule gamma. End of line could possibly be τη χ[ειρι].
  • Fragment 1 recto, line 6: Treu notes that ]κυσεν could be ειλ]κυσεν if κυσεν is orthographically correct, pointing to Jn 12:32. For σταυροθις, read σταυροθεις.
  • Fragment 1 recto, line 7: For υμες read υμεις.
  • Fragment 1 recto, line 8: For ενετειλατω read ενετειλατο. For βαπτισε read βαπτισαι. Cf. Mt 28:19–20.
  • Fragment 1 recto, line 9: Cf. Mt 28:19–20.
  • Fragment 1 recto, line 10: Cf. Mt 28:19–20.
