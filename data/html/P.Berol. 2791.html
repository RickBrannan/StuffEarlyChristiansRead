… with gladness you call the … glorifying him, the Master Christ, that appeared … For God will not ever cast those who hope in him, just as even David the musician says, that “In affliction I called upon the Lord and he heard me, (bringing me) into broad spaces,” and that “The Lord is a helper to me and I will not fear. What can anyone do to me?” A(nd) that “It is good to hope in the Lord rather than to hope in rulers.” And again in another (place) he says, “The Lord shepherds me and nothing will be lacking for me,” and that “He leads me in paths of righteousness on account of his name.”
