Notes

Fragment 1 recto, line 3: For εκδιησα read εκδικησαι. Also, φαος = φως (or φάιος?).

Fragment 1 recto, line 4: Is εδ̣[.].κατε some sort of Aorist (2nd) Active Indicative 2nd Person Plural verb? For τιχος αρηκτον read τειχος αρρηκτον; it could be a reference to the gates of Hades (cf. Mt 16:18)

Fragment 1 verso, line 3: For κεφαλαιω see Mart. Poly. 20.1 and Hermas, Vision V, 5.
