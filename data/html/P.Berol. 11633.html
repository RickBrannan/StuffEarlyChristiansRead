And he rejoiced upon the holy pool

For in the midst of the earth and heaven the Christ who confesses

And the enemies being treated arrogantly …

For the glory of the Lord encircled him

And his power overshadowed you

Shout aloud to the Lord all the earth

Because he appeared upon the earth, who God before the ages, who was the Word

Come, we will rejoice exceedingly and let us celebrate

Come, we should encounter the bridegroom, the one in Bethlehem

The one with the Father and the Holy Spirit

He was invited to the wedding as a man and the water was changed into wine

Who the blind one in Siloam received sight from

Who the leper by his word was cleansed

From five loaves into five thousands were satisfied

He went into the water of the Jordan

The lamb of God who takes away the sin of the world

And was baptized by the forerunner

And the voice from the Father saying,

“This one is my beloved Son in whom I am well pleased.”~

We were filled with great joy upon seeing the Jordan

Having been birthed upon earth as a man in him having arrived

And the forerunner himself listened to your voice saying,

“Let us complete the plans of the Father.”

“Like the Lord wanted,” said John

You, Christ, came down into the water

The mountains leaped like rams

And hills as a lamb of the sheep

having gone up of you from the Jordan

voice has come from the sky to you

“This is my beloved Son in whom I am well pleased.

Fear him.”~
