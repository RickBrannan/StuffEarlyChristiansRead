Notes

Fragment 1 recto, line 4: Likely that εμαυ[τ] is a form of εμαυτου.

Fragment 1 recto, line 19: Likely that πρεσβ[...] is a form of πρεσβυτ-.

Fragment 1 recto, line 21: Treu and Diethart’s transcription lists two oversized glyphs shaped like a seven (7), I used “77” to represent them.
