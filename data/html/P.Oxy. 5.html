Notes

  • Fragment 1 recto, line 1: Blumell and Wayment supply [ο αγελ-] at the end of the line. They view lines 1–9 (ο αγελλος .. θεοτητος) as a quotation of Hermas, Man. XI, 9–10 (43.9–10).
  • Fragment 1 recto, line 2: See Hermas, Man. XI, 9 (43.9).
  • Fragment 1 recto, line 4: Blumell and Wayment suggest a reconstruction of π[λη]ρ[οι τον ανθρωπ]ον, based on the Hermas passage, which they include in their translation.
  • Fragment 1 recto, line 7: For βούλετε read βόυλεται.
  • Fragment 1 recto, line 8: For ἔστε read ἔσται. Also see Hermas, Man. XI, 10 (43.10).
  • Fragment 1 verso, line 14: See Mt 22:43
