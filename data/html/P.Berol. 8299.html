Notes

  • Fragment 1 verso, line 1: See Lu 15:5
  • Fragment 1 verso, line 3: For ενωσας read ηνωσας(?).
  • Fragment 1 verso, line 4: See Heb 13:20
  • Fragment 1 verso, line 6: εσχα is the Imperfect Active Indicative 3rd person singular of σχάζω (according to Perseus)
  • Fragment 1 verso, line 7: See Jn 10:9
  • Fragment 3 verso, line 15: Or possibly χαριτι τη ση
  • Fragment 4 verso, line 27: For απορρητου possibly read ἀπερειτου
