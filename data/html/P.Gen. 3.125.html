… of Jesus. If he has trust … whole yourself he will give … Who makes known to you his house does not believe in you? Who gave you permission of his money box, you unbelievers? But neither will he put his ring onto your hand if you may be an unbeliever (that?) the extraordinary fruit of the spirit is faith. This spirit is born beyond you. But if an unbeliever, you may be cut off; as the unfruitful tree you will be thrown into the fire. Weep (for) yourself such as this, curse me not to teach. I learned to bless this one who speakks to those turning away from the truth and refusing to believe. But you, O fellow believers, those who are present along side are those who bring a message of peace to you. For she is reliable to you, against whom my Lord Jesus spoke when the hands start upon the house that one, even the son of peace there, that place (where) your peace dwells, but if not will return to you.
