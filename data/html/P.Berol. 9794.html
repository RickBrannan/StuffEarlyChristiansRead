… turning toward him, splendor of all dizzying, planter of all the sowing, harvester of all inner thoughts of human dimension, have mercy on me, Lord, and equip my temple, being worthy. Do not observe my transgressions. For if you observe my ungodliness, I will not be able to stand before you. But according to your great mercy and according to the magnitude of your compassions, blot out the errors through only your most-holy child Jesus Christ our Lord and Great Doctor of our souls. Might your glory, power, all majesty, (and) magnificence be upon him into all the un-aging, un-ending ages of the ages, amen.
