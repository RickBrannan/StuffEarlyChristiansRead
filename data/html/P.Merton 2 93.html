Notes

  • Fragment 1 recto, line 16: Editors note: “The writer began with οι λοιποι but changed it to the more precise οι αλλοι.”
  • Fragment 1 recto, line 26: Editors also suggest that instead of [τοις φιλ]οις the text could also be [τοις υμετερ]οις, [τοις φιλτατ]οις.
  • Fragment 1 recto, line 27: For ισελθε read εισελθε.
  • Fragment 1 recto, line 31: For ειμερωτερα read ημερωτερα.
  • Fragment 1 recto, line 34: Lines 34–35, the last lines of the letter, are written in the right margin of the papyrus at a 90 degree angle to the rest of the text of the papyrus.
